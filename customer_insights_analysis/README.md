## Key Insights

* **79.9%** of registered users made at least one purchase. 
* Top-spending customer IDs: **99148, 82305, 48605**. 
* **Men spend more per order** on average than women (**$91.4 vs $81.1**). 
* **Millennial Men** and **Gen X Men** generated the highest total spend. 
* **Millennial Women** and **Gen X Women** placed the most orders. 
* Most popular category pairs:

  * *Millennial & Gen X Women:* **Intimates, Dresses**
  * *Millennial & Gen X Men:* **Jeans, Tops/Tees/Underwear** 

These insights highlight distinct behaviors between high-spending and high-volume customersâ€”useful for personalized targeting.

## Marketing Recommendations

Grounded in behavioral data and consumer psychology:

* **Millennials:**
  Personalization, SEO, cross-device content, video marketing, authentic messaging.

* **Gen X:**
  Multichannel approach (email + Facebook + YouTube), loyalty programs, hybrid digital/traditional communication.

* **Baby Boomers:**
  Search-friendly content, simple design, benefit-focused messaging, strong customer service.

* **Gen Z:**
  TikTok & Instagram, influencer content, community-driven messaging, inclusive brand values.

These recommendations are aligned with both internal data and external consumer research.
